Who is becas?

Becas was my 11 year old dog that I've had since 2014, I grew up with her ever since I was a kid and she meant the whole world to me, she always looked up to me and I always felt thankful for having a little animal friend by my side at all times, protecting me, etc. However, in October of 2025, she started getting weaker by the day, which made me and my family worry a lot, so we tried our best to comfort her, as we didn't how long she could last, and we didn't also have any money for any vet trips at the time. Unfortunately, she died in November of 2025. May her rest in peace.
